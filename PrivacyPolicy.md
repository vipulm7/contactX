Rest assured that I take your privacy seriously, and I only use the information you provide us to improve your experience with ContactX.

## Permissions Required

ContactX requires the following permissions to provide its services:

- **Call:** I need this permission to allow users to call a number directly from the app.
- **Read Call Log:** I need this permission to know the number of incoming and outgoing calls and then search the number in the offline database to show the popup of contact's name. I also need this permission to retrieve call logs from the user's device.
- **Read Phone State:** I need this permission to notify the user as soon as a call is made or received.
- **Read Contacts:** I need this permission to read contacts for the purpose of adding them to ContactX's database.
- **Write Contacts:** I need this permission to add or delete contacts from the user's phonebook directly from the app.
- **System Alert Window:** I need this permission to show a popup to the user when a call is made or received.
- **Foreground Service:** I need this permission to let ContactX run in the background all the time. **Please note that _this won't drain your battery_, as the app isn't connected to servers.**
- **Internet:** I need this permission to let user sign in to their Google account. I also use this permission to upload and download the database file to and from Google Drive. I also use this permission to import, add and delete contacts from your Google account.
- **Get Accounts:** I need this permission to get a list of all the accounts logged into the user's phone.
